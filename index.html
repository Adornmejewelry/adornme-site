Why the Bleeding Heart

Nature already drew the symbol

The bleeding heart (Lamprocapnos spectabilis) is a bloom that holds a flower and a heart at once. Mira, the founder, chose it as Adornme's signature design because it carries the brand's whole language in a single shape.

From the bleeding heart, a pearl is born — pain transforming into beauty, softness into elegance.

The Founder

Dr. Mira "Mehraein" Mokhberi

Mehraein — a Persian name meaning "the way of the sun" and "the way of kindness."

Adornme was born from a love story and soul polished through pain, rebirth, growth, and the quiet beauty of becoming. Created by a woman who believes jewelry should be a daily reminder of who we are, what we love, and what we devote ourselves to.

"I started Adornme because I wanted to wear my own story of becoming. The symbols I couldn't find anywhere else. Pieces that hold more than metal — inviting others to make them their own."

— Mira

Dr. Mira Mokhberi, Founder · Newport Beach, California
