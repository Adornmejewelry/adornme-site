The Bleeding Heart

Why a flower whose blossoms are heart-shaped

The bleeding heart (Lamprocapnos spectabilis) was not chosen for marketing reasons. It was chosen because nature already drew it — a single bloom that holds the flower and the heart simultaneously. From the bleeding heart, a pearl is born — pain transforming into beauty, softness into elegance. The brand's permanent signature.

The Devotion Collection

For those who carry faith alongside their love story

The Devotion Collection grew naturally from the brand's language. The cross, Christ, and any sacred symbol of devotion — worn as reminders of divine love, and the peace, grace, and beauty that flow from it. Five pieces today — from the crucified Christ to the risen one, from the cross softened by a flower to the cross at rest above an open heart. More to come, across all the symbols of devotion that women already keep close.
